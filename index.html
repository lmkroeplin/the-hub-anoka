More Than a Boutique — We're a Community Hub

Jessica and Jordan Soderman purchased the building in May 2022, spent the summer renovating it, and Jessica got to work creating something special — planning every detail from finishes and fixtures to the perfect product mix.

Best friend and co-visionary Linda Gilbertson came alongside Jess to bring it all to life. Together, with their team and God's guidance, The Hub was born.

"We want to be the hub for the community. Come to be inspired, find the perfect gift with meaning, make your house feel cozy & calm, gather with friends at our events, and connect with us."

We truly love the community we're in and enjoy connecting with everyone who walks through our doors. The Hub isn't just a store — it's a gathering place.

Partner With Us

We have an event space that we love to rent to other small businesses so they can share their talents with our community. If you offer a class and would like to hold your event at The Hub, we'd love to hear from you.

  • Room rental: $190 for 3 hours ($100 for each additional 3-hr slot)
  • Classes during business hours: Sun 12–4pm, Tues–Sat 11am–5pm
  • We book 3 months out — links required 1 month before your class
  • Provide a registration link for customers to sign up
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to set up
  • Leave the space as clean as you found it (30% refund if not)

Frequently Asked Questions

What are The Hub's hours?
Sunday 12–4pm, Tuesday through Saturday 11am–5pm. We are closed on Mondays.
Where is The Hub located?
623 E Main St, Anoka, MN 55303 — right in the heart of downtown Anoka on Main Street.
What does The Hub sell?
Home decor, meaningful gifts, women's clothing, self-care items, accessories, and faith-based products. We're constantly bringing in new lines and rotating our displays — so there's always something fresh to discover.
Can I host an event at The Hub?
Yes! We rent our event space to local businesses for workshops, classes, book launches, and more. Room rental is $190 for 3 hours. Check out our Partner With Us section for full details and requirements.
Does The Hub offer gift cards?
Yes — e-gift cards are available for purchase online. The perfect gift when you're not sure what to pick!
